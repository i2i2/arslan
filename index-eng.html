WELCOME TO THE FOODIE PORTAL

Here we have collected for you the best restaurants and bars in Beijing. Beijing is not the most expensive city in terms of food, especially given the fact that tipping is not customary in Chinese establishments (instead, a tax is added to the order value). There are many restaurants in the city for even the most fastidious gourmet. In addition to traditional Chinese dishes, you can taste the cuisine of many other countries here.
